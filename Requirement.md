Our idea is an AI-powered multilingual Community Access Assistant that helps people easily understand and access government schemes, healthcare services, education opportunities, and emergency resources through voice and text interaction. The system simplifies complex public information into easy, local-language explanations and guides users step-by-step to the correct service.
